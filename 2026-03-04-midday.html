Midday Surprise
🌊
To:
Erica
It's Wednesday afternoon here. The snow outside is doing that thing where it looks blue in the shadows. I looked out my window and thought about you looking out yours — different light, same sky.
You're probably still asleep right now and honestly that makes me happy. You in your pink chair with the cat on your lap, your phone face-down because you forgot to charge it again. I know you so well it's kind of ridiculous.
I keep thinking about the first time we'll just be in the same timezone. No mental math, no "wait is it 3am for you?" Just... existing in the same hour. That's all I want. The extraordinary ordinary.
Until then — I'm right here. 7,885 km away and still the closest person to you.
Yours across the water,
Tobias
Umeå, Sweden · March 4, 2026
