Afternoon Surprise
36
days until April 9th
Thirty-six days sounds like a lot.
But every one of them is just me
getting closer to you.

I was thinking about how weird this is. Not bad weird. The kind of weird where you meet someone through a screen and suddenly they're the first person you want to talk to in the morning and the last voice you want to hear at night.

You're over there with your cat in your pink chair, probably demolishing someone in a game right now. And I'm here, 8,000 km away, smiling like an idiot because you sent me a selfie three hours ago.

36 more days of "almost." And then it's just... us. No screens. No timezone math. Just you, me, and probably your cat judging me from across the room.

I can't wait.

— T ♡
