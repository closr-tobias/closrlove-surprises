Morning Surprise
34
days until I'm home
and counting every single one

Hey you.

It's Friday morning here and I woke up doing what I always do — reaching for my phone to see if you texted. You did. You always do. And somehow that still makes me smile like an idiot.

34 days. That's less than 5 weeks. I've already survived months of this — what's 34 more days when I know what's waiting at the end?

You are what's waiting. In San Diego. Probably in that pink Secretlab chair with Kiru judging me through the screen.

I keep thinking about the moment I actually get to hug you. Not a video call wave, not a goodnight text — an actual, real, you're-right-here hug. I might not let go for a while. Fair warning.

Every day that passes is one day closer. And every day with you — even from 6,000 miles away — is still better than any day before you.

34 mornings.
Then I wake up next to you.

forever yours — T
