## 5 V2 Block Ideas

### 1. 🔐 Locket
**Object:** Gold heart-shaped locket on velvet surface, chain coiled around it
**Interaction:** Drag the tiny clasp to unlatch. Locket swings open on a hinge — two halves. Left: engraved pattern. Right: message fades in letter by letter, as if inscribed by an invisible hand. Chain settles with physics.
**Visual:** Deep burgundy velvet bg, warm gold with scratches and patina, soft spotlight, chain links with micro-shadows.
**Why it works:** Lockets are the most personal jewelry. Opening one is inherently intimate — you're seeing what someone keeps closest to their heart.
**Technical:** Clasp drag threshold. Open: two-panel rotateY with perspective-origin at hinge. Text: sequential opacity with y-drift per letter. Chain: SVG path with spring settle.

---

### 2. 🏮 Paper Lantern
**Object:** A single sky lantern sitting unlit on dark ground
**Interaction:** Hold to light the base — flame grows, paper glows warm amber. Release — lantern lifts off slowly, drifting upward. Message appears written on the lantern surface, glowing through the paper. Camera follows it into a starry sky until it joins distant lights.
**Visual:** Near-black ground, warm orange glow expanding, parallax star layers, soft bokeh dots as distant lanterns.
**Why it works:** Lantern releases are ceremonial and emotional. The rising motion creates hope. Distinct from hot air balloon (ground-to-sky release vs. floating journey).
**Technical:** Hold timer lights flame (gradient + flicker). Release triggers translateY + scale-down over 4s. Message: mix-blend-mode screen on surface. Background scroll creates parallax.

---

### 3. 📿 Worry Stone
**Object:** A smooth river stone on a dark surface, faintly luminescent
**Interaction:** Rub back and forth (swipe repeatedly). Each pass polishes — surface gets smoother, shinier, warmer. After ~5-6 passes, stone becomes translucent, revealing message glowing inside like trapped light. Final state: gentle heartbeat pulse.
**Visual:** Dark slate surface, stone with realistic texture that progressively smooths, inner glow from cold blue to warm amber. Subtle particle dust on each rub.
**Why it works:** Repetitive touch is meditative. The reveal is earned through patience. Worry stones are comfort objects — "your words comfort me."
**Technical:** Touch-move counter. Each threshold: swap texture opacity, increase inset box-shadow. Translucency via reducing bg opacity + revealing inner text. Dust particles at touch point.

---

### 4. 🗝️ Music Box Key
**Object:** Ornate brass wind-up key protruding from a dark wooden music box
**Interaction:** Rotate the key by dragging in circles (winding motion). Each turn produces a subtle mechanical click (vibration). After several winds, lid slowly opens — inside, a tiny paper scroll unfurls with the message. Golden "note" particles float upward like visible music.
**Visual:** Dark mahogany wood grain, brass key with verdigris patina, red velvet interior, warm lamplight, floating golden particles.
**Why it works:** Music boxes are pure nostalgia. Winding is tactile and rewarding. No audio needed — visual particles sell it. Distinct from deleted music-box (which was audio-dependent).
**Technical:** Circular gesture via atan2 angle tracking. Key: rotate transform synced to gesture. Lid: rotateX on hinge. Scroll: height animation with overflow hidden. Particles: sine-wave drift upward.

---

### 5. 🪶 Quill & Ink
**Object:** Feather quill resting across a brass inkwell, blank parchment below
**Interaction:** Tap the quill — it dips into ink (dunk animation, ink drips). Moves to parchment and writes the message in flowing cursive, stroke by stroke, ghost-hand guided. Ink glistens wet, then dries to matte. Final flourish underlines the last word.
**Visual:** Candlelit desk, aged parchment with burnt edges, glossy black ink with specular highlights that fade as it "dries," feather with barb detail via CSS gradients.
**Why it works:** Watching handwriting appear is mesmerizing. The quill-dip creates ceremony. Ink drying is a tiny satisfying detail. Feels like a letter from another century.
**Technical:** Quill translateY dip + rotate. Writing: SVG stroke-dashoffset animation per word. Wet ink: gradient highlight fading per letter. Flourish: final SVG path with slower timing.
