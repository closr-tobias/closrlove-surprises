## 5 V2 Block Ideas

### 1. 🔭 Telescope
**Object:** Brass telescope on a dark starlit balcony
**Interaction:** Drag a circular viewport across the night sky. Most of the sky is dark/blurred — the viewfinder reveals sharp stars and detail. Hidden message fragments only appear inside the lens. Find them all → telescope lowers, full message appears etched on a brass plaque.
**Visual:** Deep navy sky, film grain, warm brass rim on viewfinder, subtle parallax on star layers.
**Why it works:** Discovery through exploration. Small viewport creates anticipation. Each found word is a reward.
**Technical:** CSS `clip-path: circle()` following touch. Two layers — blurred bg + sharp detail only inside clip. Message fragments at fixed coords, opacity on proximity.

---

### 2. 🕯️ Candle
**Object:** Single candle on a dark surface, flame flickering
**Interaction:** Long-press the flame — it sputters, flickers harder, dies. In the rising smoke trail, the message materializes letter by letter, drifting upward like smoke particles. Room dims to near-black, only smoke-message glows.
**Visual:** Photorealistic candle (CSS layered gradients + blur), dark wood table, rising smoke particles.
**Why it works:** Blowing out a candle is primal. Message in smoke is poetic and surprising.
**Technical:** Long-press triggers flame death sequence. Flame: stacked CSS gradients with keyframe wiggle. Smoke: 30-40 small divs with upward drift + horizontal wobble + fade. Letters positioned along smoke path.

---

### 3. 🧭 Compass
**Object:** Vintage brass compass, glass face, slightly weathered
**Interaction:** Needle spins freely. Tilt phone (or drag on desktop) to align. Instead of north, needle seeks the message. As user aligns — needle steadies, glass fog clears, engraved message revealed beneath the face. Closer = brighter glow.
**Visual:** Dark leather bg, brass compass with glass reflection, light caustics. Needle has spring physics — overshoots and settles.
**Why it works:** "You're my north" implicit without saying it. Needle settling is deeply satisfying.
**Technical:** DeviceOrientation for tilt (fallback: drag). Spring physics on needle. Glass fog via backdrop-filter reducing on alignment. Engraving with mix-blend-mode overlay + gold gradient.

---

### 4. ✉️ Wax Stamp Envelope
**Object:** Rich parchment envelope sealed with a wax stamp
**Interaction:** Press and hold the wax seal. It warms — color shifts from solid burgundy to molten, edges soften. After 2-3s the seal cracks, envelope unfolds in stages. Each fold reveals a line of the message on aged paper. Final fold shows a small drawn symbol.
**Visual:** Cream parchment texture, deep red wax with embossed initial, candlelight warmth, paper shadow layers.
**Why it works:** Hold-to-warm creates patience and care. Unfolding builds anticipation line by line.
**Technical:** Wax: radial gradient + filter shift on hold timer. Crack: clip-path split animation. Folds: rotateX with perspective, sequenced delays. Lines fade per fold. Vibrate on crack.

---

### 5. 🪞 Pocket Mirror
**Object:** Ornate vintage pocket mirror, closed
**Interaction:** Tap to open (swings with a satisfying click). Instead of reflection — shimmering liquid mercury surface. Swipe across it — ripples clear to reveal message underneath, word by word. Fully revealed → frame glows warmly.
**Visual:** Dark velvet bg, gold filigree frame, mercury-silver liquid with ripple physics, warm ambient glow.
**Why it works:** Mirrors are intimate. Liquid mercury is unexpected and mesmerizing.
**Technical:** Open: rotateY with perspective. Mercury: Canvas 2D ripple heightmap. Swipe clears via destination-out compositing. Frame glow: layered box-shadow on completion.
