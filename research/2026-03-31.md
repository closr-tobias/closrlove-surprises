## 5 V2 Block Ideas

### 1. 🧭 Pocket Watch
**Object:** Antique brass pocket watch, closed, resting on dark leather. Chain trailing off-screen.
**Interaction:** Tap the crown button to pop the case open with a satisfying spring. Watch face revealed — hands spin backward rapidly, then slow to a stop. The dial face dissolves into frosted glass, message appears behind it as if engraved on the back case. Subtle tick vibration on open.
**Visual:** Aged brass with micro-scratches, roman numerals, dark leather surface with grain, warm amber spotlight. Blued steel hands. Engraved-line texture behind the text.
**Why it works:** Pocket watches carry "timeless" weight. Turning back time is emotional shorthand for "I'd choose you again." The spring-open is a tactile payoff.
**Technical:** Crown tap triggers rotateX case open with spring ease. Hands: rotate fast→slow via cubic-bezier. Dissolve: clip-path circle expanding. Text: letter-spacing tightens as opacity fades in.

---

### 2. 🪵 Carved Tree Trunk
**Object:** Close-up of dark tree bark, a small bare patch where bark has been stripped. A knife tip visible at the edge.
**Interaction:** Trace your finger to "carve" — follow a faint dotted outline. Wood shavings curl away and the carved groove appears behind your touch, raw pale wood against dark bark. Completing the carve reveals initials inside a heart or a short message. Sap beads along the grooves.
**Visual:** Ultra-detailed bark texture, warm dappled forest light, pale heartwood contrast, tiny wood curl particles. Sap has golden translucency.
**Why it works:** Carving initials into a tree is the oldest love note. The trace interaction makes it personal — you're the one carving.
**Technical:** SVG path follow with proximity detection. Groove: stroke-dashoffset reveal. Wood shavings: particle divs with rotate + fall. Sap: delayed opacity blobs along path.

---

### 3. 🔦 Flashlight in the Dark
**Object:** Total darkness. A flashlight lying on its side, faint metallic glint.
**Interaction:** Tap flashlight to pick it up — screen becomes a beam of light following your finger. The message is written on a wall in the dark, but you can only see what the beam illuminates. Sweep to read word by word. Dust particles float through the beam.
**Visual:** True black except warm white cone. Concrete wall texture. Text looks spray-painted or chalk-drawn. Volumetric dust motes.
**Why it works:** The search creates tension and discovery. Reading piece by piece makes each word land harder. "You're my light" without saying it.
**Technical:** Radial gradient mask follows touch/pointer. Text always rendered but clipped by light mask. Dust particles with opacity tied to beam distance. Subtle parallax on move.

---

### 4. 🧊 Ice Block Melt
**Object:** A solid block of blue-white ice on a dark surface, something faintly visible trapped inside.
**Interaction:** Hold your thumb against the ice — warmth melts it. Frost recedes from touch point in rings, cracks appear, water droplets run down. Gradually reveals a folded note frozen inside. When fully melted, note unfolds in a puddle, message readable.
**Visual:** Translucent ice with internal fractures and air bubbles, cold blue shifting to warm amber. Water pooling with reflections. Note slightly waterlogged, ink slightly bled but legible.
**Why it works:** "Melting someone's cold exterior" is visceral. Holding to warm is intimate. Slow reveal builds anticipation.
**Technical:** Hold timer drives melt progress. Ice: layered divs with blur + opacity reduction. Cracks: SVG paths at thresholds. Water: expanding puddle via border-radius morph. Note: scale + rotateX unfold at final stage.

---

### 5. 🎐 Wind Chime
**Object:** Metal tubes hanging from a wooden bar, silhouetted against twilight sky. Still and silent.
**Interaction:** Swipe across to create "wind" — chimes sway and collide, each tube catching light as it moves. Each collision reveals a word, appearing etched on the tubes, briefly glowing. Full message assembles below as words accumulate. Chimes gradually settle.
**Visual:** Deep indigo-to-coral gradient sky, silhouette wooden mount, brushed metal tubes with specular reflections, golden collision flashes. Words glow warm then cool to white.
**Why it works:** Wind chimes evoke porches, warm evenings, peace. Swipe feels like conjuring a breeze. Word-by-word assembly through motion is poetic.
**Technical:** Tubes: pendulum physics via transform-origin top + sinusoidal animation. Collision detection between adjacent tubes. Word reveal: opacity flash at collision. Settle: damped oscillation.

## Notes

- Ice melt vs worry stone (Mar 30): ice has transformation stages vs stone's texture polish — different enough
- Carved tree: only "trace a path" interaction — complements tap/hold/swipe blocks
- Pocket watch vs locket: similar open-to-reveal but time-reversal animation differentiates
