Chapter One

The Boy in the Snow

Once, in a small town wrapped in winter — where the northern lights danced like secrets across the sky — there lived a boy who built things with his hands. He welded steel by day and dreamed in code by night, and though his life was full, there was a quiet space inside him shaped exactly like someone he hadn't met yet.

Chapter Two

The Girl with the Cat

Across the world, where the Pacific kissed the California shore, a girl sat in the glow of her screen with a ridiculously fluffy white cat on her lap. She had a laugh that could restart a frozen heart and a mind that saw beauty where others saw blank canvas. She didn't know it yet, but the universe was already plotting.

Chapter Three

The Distance Between

The distance was 8,574 kilometers — a number that would terrify most people. But love, as it turns out, is a terrible listener. It doesn't care about time zones or passport stamps. It heard two hearts tuning to the same frequency and thought, "Ah, there you are."

Chapter Four

Late Nights & Bright Screens

They fell into a rhythm only they understood. His 3AM was her 6PM. Her good morning was his goodnight. They built a world inside the glow of their screens — a world where distance was just a word, not a wall. And every night, before sleep pulled him under, he'd whisper into the phone: "Goodnight, my love."
