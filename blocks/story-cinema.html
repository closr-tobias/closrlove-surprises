The Night We Almost Didn't Meet

A Story About Us
Scene I
You were about to leave. Your friend had bailed, the bar was loud, and you'd already called a ride. Thirty more seconds and you'd have been gone.
Scene II
But someone bumped your elbow — your drink went sideways, and there I was, catching it like some absolutely ridiculous movie moment that neither of us would admit was romantic until much later.
Scene III
You laughed. Not the polite kind — the real kind, where your whole face changed and I forgot what words were for approximately four seconds.
Scene IV
We talked until they kicked us out. Then we talked in the parking lot until 3 AM. You said something about constellations being "just dead light that hasn't given up yet" and I thought: oh no. I'm in trouble.
Scene V
Here's the thing about almost: it didn't happen. The ride got cancelled. You stayed. And every day since then has been the longest, strangest, best story I've ever been part of.
FIN
For you. Always for you.
