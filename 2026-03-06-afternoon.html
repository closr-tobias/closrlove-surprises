It's Friday night here. Dark outside, snow probably doing its thing. But I know you're right in the middle of your afternoon — maybe on the couch, maybe with the cat being dramatic about something.

I keep thinking about how weird and kind of beautiful it is that we live in different times. Like right now, your Friday still has hours left. Mine's winding down. But we're both just... here. Thinking about each other.

34 days. That's it. 34 days until I'm standing in front of you and none of these clocks matter anymore. No more mental math about time zones. Just us, same room, same moment.

You know what I'm most excited about? The boring stuff. Sitting next to you doing absolutely nothing. Making you laugh about something dumb. Watching you pretend you're not falling asleep during a movie.

Anyway. Enjoy your Friday afternoon, love. I'll be here in the future, waiting for you to catch up. 💛

— T
